About AIMBTI

AIMBTI is an innovative iOS application that combines the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) personality assessment with cutting-edge AI technology. Our mission is to help people better understand themselves and improve their personal and professional relationships.

With our AI-powered personal advisor, users receive tailored guidance based on their unique personality type, helping them navigate life's challenges and make better decisions aligned with their natural strengths and preferences.
